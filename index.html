Welcome

Based on the SONYC Sensors Map, its easy to conclude we live in an extremely loud and auditory world. Now, imagine if your participation in this auditory world was hampered by a hearing disability.

That would be a huge change, right?

Using CDC Hearing Loss data from AWS Data Exchange, we developed a chloropleth map showing the estimated people per 100 who have a hearing disability in each state.

Click "Begin" to be brought to the map and view the data for yourself. Also, don't forget to use the buttons in the left to page through different age groups.
